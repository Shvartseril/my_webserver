The first computer game named "TIC-TAC-TOE":

The very first computer game was released in 1952 and became the prototype of the tic-tac-toe board game. Tic-Tac-Toe was a 3x3 square field, the player made a move, in response, the computer also made a move. It was created by A.S. Douglas and was located on a large computer. The game played a key role in the story, but still did not fully meet the definition of a video game.

game "TENNIS FOR TWO"

A few years later, a more world-famous game appeared, which was called Tennis for Two. The creator of the game turned out to be William Higinbotham, the greatest physicist and a member of the team to create a nuclear bomb. It was in the Buckhaven laboratory that the author came up with this game.

Thus, the first computer game appeared in 1958. It was demonstrated on the open day and to the amazement of the creator, it was a huge success. The development of Tennis for Two did not take much time, but it became popular among the participants of the exhibition.

game "SPACE WAR"

But progress does not stand still, and the video game industry slowly began to gain momentum. In 1962, the first computer game was developed by programmers of the Institute in Massachusetts. The first SpaceWar can be called due to the fact that it was the only game at that time developed directly for the computer. Writing this program took place on a PDP-1 machine and took a total of about 200 hours. It was for this game that the game controller was created.

game 'PONG'

Subsequently, a simple Pong arcade game was released for the machines, which also had sound effects. She received special recognition from the visitors of bars, and the owners of establishments such a slot machine for a week gave a budget of about $ 300.
